## üìñ About the Project

**QuickAId** is a comprehensive digital health ecosystem designed to bridge critical gaps in the Indian healthcare sector. By integrating real-time hospital data, AI-driven emergency management, and QR-based digital health records, QuickAId ensures faster emergency responses and smarter treatment decisions.

Our mission is to empower individuals with real-time health data and insurance insights while assisting hospitals in delivering cost-effective, life-saving healthcare.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Problem Statement

The current healthcare ecosystem faces severe inefficiencies:
* **Missing History:** Over 60% of emergency patients arrive without medical records, delaying critical treatment.
* **Insurance Opacity:** 80% of patients are unaware of which hospitals accept their insurance, leading to manual paperwork delays.
* **Poor Coordination:** Weak ambulance-hospital coordination results in avoidable delays.
* **Reactive vs. Preventive:** Lack of AI-driven systems to provide early health alerts.
